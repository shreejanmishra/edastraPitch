Key issue: At AI pipeline costs, ₹5 Cr is too much for "Grades 6–8 only." Justify it by committing to a full 6-grade × 5-subject library (~1,500 videos). That turns a cost into a content moat.
The right VC answer: "We allocate ₹5 Cr to build a 2-year content IP library across Grades 6–10, 5 core subjects — ~1,500 videos at ₹2,100–3,000 each. No competitor can replicate this for under ₹5 Cr and 18 months of production time."
Benchmark that wins rooms: Traditional studios charge ₹1,000–3,000/min. Edastra AI pipeline = ₹230–300/min. That is a 10–13x cost advantage — a structural production moat competitors cannot replicate without the same AI toolstack and team.
