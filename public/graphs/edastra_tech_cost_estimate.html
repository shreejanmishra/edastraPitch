18-month tech spend = ₹4–6 Cr out of ₹20 Cr raise. Engineering salaries dominate (₹1.9–2.7 Cr). Infra is surprisingly affordable pre-scale — AWS + Cloudflare for 200k users costs less than one mid-level engineer.
The CDN inflection point: At 500k users watching 3 videos/week, video delivery crosses ₹1.5L/month. This is when switching from AWS CloudFront to Cloudflare Stream saves ₹8–12L/year. Set a calendar reminder for Month 9.
VR adds ₹28–42L/yr extra from 2028. Budget this in the Series A deployment — not pre-seed. The PoC modules (2 × demo scenes) cost ~₹3–5L in cloud costs, not ₹28L.
